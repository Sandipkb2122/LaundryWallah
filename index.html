Welcome to Laundry Wallah

About Us :

"Laundry Walla" is your neighborhood's answer to all things laundry! We understand that in today's fast-paced world, finding time for laundry can be a chore. That's where we step in. "Laundry Walla" is a dedicated and professional laundry service committed to bringing freshness and convenience right to your doorstep.

Our Services :

  1. Wash & Fold :

    Our most popular service, perfect for everyday laundry. We carefully sort your clothes, wash them using high-quality detergents and softeners, tumble dry them on appropriate settings, and then meticulously fold and package them.

  2. Dry Cleaning :

    Specialized care for delicate fabrics and garments that require non-water-based cleaning. We use eco-friendly solvents to effectively remove stains and clean items without damaging their texture or color.

  3. Premium Wash & Iron :

    For those who prefer their clothes perfectly pressed. After a thorough wash and dry, each garment is professionally ironed to a crisp finish, ready to wear.

  4. Duvet & Blanket Cleaning:

    Large items like duvets, comforters, and blankets require special attention. We have the capacity and expertise to clean these bulky items thoroughly, ensuring they are fresh, hygienic, and fluffy.

  5. Curtain & Upholstery Cleaning :

    Extend the freshness to your home furnishings. We offer professional cleaning for curtains and select upholstery items. Our team will pick up your items and deliver them back refreshed.
